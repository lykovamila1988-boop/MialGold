## Key Takeaways

1. **Chain_ID makes financial decisions clear** — You don't ask "Should I spend on ads?" You ask "Should I scale the 162% ROI reel channel or optimize the 123% ROI ad channel?" Context changes everything.

2. **Context drives decisions** — Same $100 expense:
   - In email: ROI = -100% → PAUSE
   - In reels: ROI = 162% → INCREASE
   - In ads: ROI = 123% → OPTIMIZE

3. **Financial decision is automatic** — Once you know ROI:
   - ROI > 300% = double the budget
   - ROI 100-300% = maintain
   - ROI 0-100% = fix or pause
   - ROI < 0% = kill it now

4. **Дима's job** — Provide the chain data, the ROI, and the financial context. You make the decision, but from a position of knowledge.
